1) What is SSH?
>  SSH, also known as Secure Shell  is a network communication protocol that enables two computers to communicate(hypertext transfer protocol , which is the protocol used to transfer hypertext such as web pages) and share data.

  SSH connections have mostly been used to secure different types of communications between local machine and a remote host, including:
. Secure a remote access to resources.
. Remote execution of commands.
. Interactive and automated file transfer.
. Delivery of software patches and updates.
